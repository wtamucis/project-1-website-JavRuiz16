Welcome to the Dirt

This site is a practical companion for exploring singletrack—designed for runners stepping off the road and into the wild. You’ll find actionable basics, approachable training ideas, and pointers on gear that actually matters. The goal is confidence: read a bit, lace up, and go.

Trails vary wildly: smooth pine‑needle paths, rocky limestone ledges, sandy washes. Understanding surface and grade helps you pace smartly. Expect hikes on steep climbs, quick steps on descents, and an even effort rather than a fixed pace.
